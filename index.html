About US

We are a group of researchers, freelancers, and companies with a common interest on distributed systems (especially blockchain), data processing, and developing IT solutions for different application domains in Vietnam and the world.

We establish this project in order to foster joint effort between academics and industries in the development and spreading of blockchain technology as well as bringing effective IT solutions for real life aspects. The collaboration and project thus operate under spin-off model from Hanoi University of Science and Technology.

Our main goal is to build a platform that allows developing and deploying decentralized applications in a very easy way. The platform creates a unique ecosystem over blockchain networks. Three first productions in the ecosystem include bk-wallet and bk-agriledger and bk-certificate.

About US

We are a group of researchers, freelancers, and companies with a common interest on distributed systems (especially blockchain), data processing, and developing IT solutions for different application domains in Vietnam and the world.

We establish this project in order to foster joint effort between academics and industries in the development and spreading of blockchain technology as well as bringing effective IT solutions for real life aspects. The collaboration and project thus operate under spin-off model from Hanoi University of Science and Technology.

Our main goal is to build a platform that allows developing and deploying decentralized applications in a very easy way. The platform creates a unique ecosystem over blockchain networks. Three first productions in the ecosystem include bk-wallet and bk-agriledger and bk-certificate.

Our Products

BkWallet

Our hardware wallet helps protect your private keys in a very secure way. Just plug it into a smart phone and sign your transaction or smart contract.

V-Chain Platform

Our blockchain-based platform provides a solution to keep IoT, certificate, or any data on a desired blockchain network. It supports REST APIs (push, get, trace, visualize).

Consensus models

Business process is different for each application, so that's why there is no consensus model satisfying all. We are doing research consensus models for different DApps.

Our R&D Projects

V-Chain

We focus on building a blockchain-based platform that enables application developers to create in-an-easy-way their decentralized applications.

V-Chain Project
We focus on building a blockchain-based platform that enables application developers to create in-an-easy-way their decentralized applications.
To demonstrate the capability of the proposed platform, two tracking and tracing applications will be developed: one for agricultural product traceability and the other for certificate authentication. Besides, several research problems also are addressed in this project such as user identity, data mapping structure, smart contract programming model, consensus algorithms, data privacy, performance, as well as trading paradigm.
Principal Investigator: Nguyen Binh Minh
Funding agency: VIN Innovation Foundation

AgriBKChain

We propose a blockchain-based network, namely AgriBKChain, to solve challenges of trusted traceability and advertising for agricultural products.

AgriBKChain Project
We propose a blockchain based network, namely AgriBKChain, to solve challenges of trusted traceability and advertising for agricultural products. We identify two objectives of our project:
1. Providing trusted information of origin and culture of agricultural products, such as location identification, production diary, harvesting and storing processes, and how to serve.
2. Connecting to existing networks in Japan, Europe, and US for product advertising and export. For example, blockchain-based EXA network in Fukuoka, Japan and TE-FOOD in Germany.
Principal Investigator: Dao Thanh Chung
Funding agency: ICT Virtual Organization of ASEAN Institutes and NICT (ASEAN IVO)
