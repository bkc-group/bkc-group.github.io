About US

We are a group of researchers, freelancers, and companies with a common interest on blockchain foundations, and solutions for different application domains in Vietnam and worldwide. We establish this project in order to foster joint effort between academics and industries in the development and spreading of blockchain technology as well as bringing effective solutions for real life aspects. Our collaboration and project follow spin-off model from Hanoi University of Science and Technology.

Our main goal is to build a platform that allows developing and deploying decentralized applications in a very easy way. The platform creates a unique ecosystem over blockchain networks. Three first productions in the ecosystem include bk-wallet and bk-agriledger and bk-certificate.
